BABY FOOD

2-4 MONTHS

mother milk

4-6 MONTHS

Check with pediatrician first;

start tastes of cereals, fruits and vegetables.

6-8 MONTHS

Main source of nutrition: Breast milk or formula

1/4 cup of grains/cereals, twice a day.

2 tablespoons of vegetables puree, twice a day.

1 tablespoons of meat or poultry puree,twice a day.

8-10 MONTHS

Main source of nutrition: Breast milk or formula

1/2 cup of vegetables per day, offering a variety.

1/2 cup of fruits per day, offering a variety.

1 ounce of meat or beans a day.

1 ounce of grains a day.

10-12 MONTHS

3 meals and 2-3 snack a day;

begin weaning form breast milk or formula to whole milk.

2 cups of dairy a day, served in 1/4 to 1/2 cup servings.

2 ounces of grains with half of the grains as whole grains.

3/4 cup of vegetables

1 cup of fruits

1.5 ounces of meaty beans,thoroughtly cooked and easy to chew.

whole milker or water to drink.
